Dill Gravy

Pan sauce

  • Flour
  • Sour Cream
  • Dill
  • Stock

Recipe Steps

  • Mix together flour, dill, and sour cream.
  • Pour stock into a pan that's just been used to shallow fry something.
  • Scrape the pan to get the fond up.
  • Pour in the flour, dill, and sour cream mixture and stir until thick.
